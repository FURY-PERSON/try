Privacy Policy

Fact Front — Mobile Application

Effective date: February 28, 2026  |  Last updated: February 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Fact Front mobile application ("App", "we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our service. By installing or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Generated Automatically

When you first launch the App, a random anonymous identifier (UUID) is generated and stored on your device. This identifier is used to associate your gameplay data with your device. It is not linked to your real identity, email, phone number, or any account on other services.

1.2 Information You Provide

  • Nickname — a display name you choose or is auto-generated (up to 16 characters). Visible on leaderboards.
  • Avatar emoji — a single emoji you select as your profile picture. Visible on leaderboards.

We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, date of birth, location, photos, contacts, or any other personal identification information.

1.3 Gameplay Data

To provide game features, leaderboards, and statistics, we collect:

  • Answers to questions (correct/incorrect)
  • Time spent on each question
  • Scores and streaks
  • Daily set and collection completion status
  • Total games played

1.4 Device and App Data

  • App settings preferences (language, theme, sound, haptics)
  • Push notification token (only if you enable notifications)
  • App launch count

1.5 Data Collected by Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may independently collect information. See Section 4 for details.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 On-Device Storage

Your settings, device ID, and game session state are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data is deleted when you uninstall the App.

3.2 Server-Side Storage

Your gameplay data (answers, scores, leaderboard entries) and profile (nickname, avatar emoji, push token) are stored on our server in a PostgreSQL database. Data is transmitted over HTTPS.

3.3 No Account System

The App uses anonymous, device-based identification. There is no registration, login, or password. If you uninstall and reinstall the App, a new identifier is generated and your previous data becomes inaccessible.

5. Advertising and Tracking

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. On iOS, we request your consent via the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before accessing your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) for personalized ads. If you decline, ads will still be shown but they will not be personalized.

On Android, the Google Advertising ID (GAID) may be used for ad personalization. You can reset or opt out of ad personalization through your device's settings (Settings → Google → Ads).

Ad frequency is limited: interstitial ads are shown no more than 10 times per day with a minimum interval of 2 minutes between them.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Information may be shared only in the following cases:

  • Leaderboards: Your nickname, avatar emoji, score, and streaks are visible to other App users on the leaderboard.
  • Third-party service providers: As described in Section 4, data is processed by Google (AdMob, Firebase) and Expo for their respective purposes.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.

7. Data Retention

  • On-device data is retained until you uninstall the App.
  • Server-side data is retained for as long as the App service is operational. Since there is no account system, we cannot automatically identify specific user data for deletion without the device identifier.

8. Your Rights and Choices

  • Push notifications: You can enable or disable notifications at any time in the App settings or your device settings.
  • Ad tracking (iOS): You can control ad tracking through the ATT prompt or in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Ad personalization (Android): You can opt out in Settings → Google → Ads.
  • Data deletion: To request deletion of your server-side data, please contact us at the email below with your approximate registration date and nickname. Uninstalling the App will delete all locally stored data.
  • Access and portability: You can request a copy of your data by contacting us.

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the GDPR, including the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, and data portability. Contact us to exercise these rights.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country of residence. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws than your country.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
